Garbage/Trash:
Items that cannot be disposed of in your kitchen disposal or household trash should be placed in the dumpster.
Furniture, TVs, large items, or construction materials are prohibited in or around the dumpster.
When the West dumpster is full, please use the East dumpster.

The Collier County Landfill (a.k.a. "the dump") for disposing of larger items is located at:
3730 White Lake Blvd
Naples, Florida
Monday - Saturday 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM

Recycling:
Recyclables must be placed in the Recycle bins.
Please do not place garbage/trash, plastic bags, or any type of styrofoam in the Recycle bins.
Boxes must be broken down before being placed in the Recycle bins. NOTE: We are charged if they are not broken down.

The Naples Recycling drop-off center for recycling larger items is located at:
2640 Corporate Flight Dr.
Naples, Florida
Monday - Saturday 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM
